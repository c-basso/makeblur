# Content Notes

## Product Positioning (Important)

- The website is a **promo landing page for the iOS app**.
- The web page `photo-blur-editor-online.html` is only for **full photo blur** (blur the entire image).
- Advanced features, including **background blur**, are part of the **iOS app experience** and should be presented as app functionality.
- In SEO/content copy, avoid implying that full background-blur editing is available in the online web tool unless explicitly supported.
- For SEO guest posts and link-building content:
  - Primary goal: promote the **iOS app** and its advanced editing value.
  - Secondary goal: earn backlinks to the site naturally within app-focused content.
  - If the article topic is background blur on iPhone, keep the core content about **how to do background blur in the app**.
  - Mention the online tool only as a **free limited alternative** (full-image blur), not the main solution.
